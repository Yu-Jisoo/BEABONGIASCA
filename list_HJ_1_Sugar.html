SUGAR, SPICE & EQUAL RIGHTS

The Sugar, Spice & Equal Rights Collection is inspired by coming of age in the 90s/early 2000s and the various female figures whom Bea grew up with, both from fiction and in real life.

Following in the footsteps of the powerful heroines of our childhood - from the Spice Girls, to Hermione Granger, Lisa Simpson and, as the collection's name suggests, the Powerpuff Girls - this generation have grown into the girl bosses and super heroines of today. Now, building on the lessons in bravery, kindness and fortitude learnt from our heroes, we are not satisfied to just be "nice".
