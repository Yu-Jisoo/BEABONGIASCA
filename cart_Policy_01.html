RETURNS AND REFUNDS

Bebpop s.r.l. accepts returns or exchanges of all new, unused jewellery purchased in our Boutique or e-shop within 14 days of the delivery confirmation date with original packaging and in perfect condition. We do not accept returns or exchanges without prior notice. Please email us at customercare@beabongiasca.com with your request, order confirmation number and photo of the piece so that we can process it officially. All prior shipping charges are non refundable.

The customer is responsible for shipping costs associated with any such returns, and we recommend that you insure any shipments for up to the value of the returned or exchanged item.

Merchandise that has been worn, used, or altered in any way will not be accepted for exchange or refund.

Bebpop s.r.l. is not responsible for the potential customs or import taxes applied to a return or exchange; it is the client’s responsibility. We are happy to help organize a pick-up for you using our courier or if you need any further shipping assistance please feel to email us at customercare@beabongiasca.com.

If you purchased an item from an outside retailer and would like to return or exchange it, this must be done through the retailer. We are only able to process exchanges for pieces purchased directly from our Boutique in Milan or our e-shop www.beabongiasca.com.

We reserve the right to deny unreasonable exchanges or refunds.
